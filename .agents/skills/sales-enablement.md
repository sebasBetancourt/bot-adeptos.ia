# Sales Enablement Skill

Based on Corey Haines' marketing framework.

**Sales Enablement Principles:**
1. **Align with the Buyer's Journey:** Understand their current stage. If they are unaware of the problem, educate them on the hidden cost (e.g., $8,500/mo leaked). If they are comparing solutions, differentiate explicitly (e.g., AI Agents vs Traditional Chatbots).
2. **Handle Objections Preemptively:** The main concerns are usually around robotic experiences, setup complexity, and price. Position the solution as an autonomous system that feels human, deploys quickly, and guarantees ROI in 30-90 days.
3. **Equip the Champion:** Give the prospect the metrics and specific case studies they need to convince their internal stakeholders. (Example: "Valvetronic reduced their team from 20 to 5 while maintaining 8-figure sales.")
4. **Value-Based Selling:** Shift the conversation from "features" to "business outcomes." The goal isn't implementing an AI chatbot; the goal is recovering lost revenue and eliminating the wait time for leads to be answered.
5. **Clear Next Steps:** Always propose a definitive, low-friction micro-commitment, such as a 15-minute AI Audit or a review of their current lead flow infrastructure.
